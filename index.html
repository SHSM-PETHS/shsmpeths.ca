Who we are

Specialist High Skills Majors (SHSM) is for motivated individuals like you. Through the program, you’ll be given to opportunity to focus on the career path that matches your skills and interests. While working towards your Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD), you’ll also be able to earn the SHSM seal on your diploma by meeting the following requirements:

Complete a specific bundle of 8-10 courses in your selected field.

Earn valuable industry certifications such as first aid and CPR qualifications.
