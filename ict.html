About The Sector

Interested in technology? Do you love programming, engineering, graphic design, or data analysis? If that sounds familiar, welcome to the Information and Communications Technology sector! Here, you’ll be able to participate in a wide array of interesting technological opportunities both in and out of school.

Implement your knowledge to participate in real-world technological advancements.

Enhance your problem solving and thinking skills through challenges.

Connect with professional programmers, engineers, designers, and more!

What are reach aheads?

Reach aheads are completed by attending community events in relation to the sector you are pursuing. This includes post-secondary presentations, an interview with an employee in the sector, a conference or workshop, and more.

What are experiential learnings?

Experiential learning certifications are gained through attending a hands on event in the sector. This includes observing an employee in the sector, a work experience, participation in a competition, and more.

What are sector partnered events?

Sector partnered events (SPE) are learning experiences developed by sector partners and teachers, focusing on innovation + creativity + entrepreneurship (ICE), coding, and mathematical literacy.

What are CO-OP placements?

Co-operative (CO-OP) refers to when a student works alongside an employer, receiving valuable experience in the field they are interested, while at the same time aiding the employer.
