2SLGBTQ+ Mental Health

Hey Trudeau! SHSM and Trudettes are hosting a 2SLGBTQ+ Mental Health event on Wednesday, December 15th from 4-5 PM on Zoom. This is a safe and supportive environment for students to participate, share, and learn about the factors that impact individuals in the 2SLGBTQ+ community. Tristan Coolman, President of Pflag York Region will talk about topics around family and his story of coming out! Andy Gayoso, a school social worker that works with 2SLGBTQ+ students will talk about assumptions, misconceptions, and identity. Anyone is welcome to join, and SHSM Health & Wellness students will earn a reach ahead. Spots are first-come, first-serve, so if you are interested in joining, you must fill in a registration form that will be posted on the SHSM classroom (code: bb7sirw) OR the Trudettes classroom (code: aljlbvb). The deadline to sign up is on December 14, 6:00 PM. We hope to see you there!
